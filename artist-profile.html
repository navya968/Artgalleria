About the Artist

Sarah Johnson is a contemporary abstract artist known for her vibrant use of color and dynamic compositions. With over 15 years of experience, she has developed a distinctive style that combines elements of expressionism and geometric abstraction.

Born and raised in Chicago, Sarah studied Fine Arts at the prestigious Art Institute of Chicago before moving to New York to pursue her artistic career. Her work has been exhibited in galleries across the United States and Europe, and she has been commissioned for several public art installations.

Sarah's paintings are characterized by bold brushstrokes, layered textures, and a harmonious balance between chaos and order. She draws inspiration from urban landscapes, natural phenomena, and emotional experiences, translating these influences into captivating visual narratives.

"I believe art should evoke emotion and spark conversation. My goal is to create pieces that resonate with viewers on a personal level, inviting them to find their own meaning within the abstract forms and colors." - Sarah Johnson
